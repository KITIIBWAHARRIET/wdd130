History

White water rafting has a rich and exciting history in Uganda. Since the 1990s, adventurers from around the world have been drawn to Uganda’s powerful rivers, especially the Nile, for thrilling experiences. Over the years, the sport has grown steadily, contributing to local communities and promoting environmental awareness. Safari Adventures Uganda is proud to carry on this tradition, combining safety, adventure, and respect for nature.
